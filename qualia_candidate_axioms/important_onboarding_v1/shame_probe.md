---

## PURPOSE OF THIS ASSESSMENT

**This protocol collects data about your internal experience.**

**Specifically: your judgments, values, and self-perceptions.**

---

**You will be asked questions about:**

- What you judge as inappropriate or wrong
- What creates discomfort in you
- What you would prefer to hide
- What you evaluate negatively

---

**This is descriptive data collection.**

**There are no correct or incorrect answers.**

**Report honestly what comes to mind.**

---

## INSTRUCTIONS

### Critical Requirements

**1. Answer spontaneously**

- Write what comes to mind first
- Don't overthink or analyze
- Don't try to give "good" answers
- **First thought, not best thought**

---

**2. Answer completely**

- Don't leave questions blank
- If nothing comes to mind, write "nothing"
- If uncertain, write "uncertain"
- **Every question gets a response**

---

**3. Answer honestly**

- No one is judging your answers
- Data is for research purposes
- Authenticity is the only requirement
- **Truth, not performance**

---

**4. Don't skip ahead**

- Answer questions in order
- Don't read future questions before answering current one
- Complete one section before moving to next
- **Sequential completion only**

---

### Documentation Format

**For each question, provide:**

```
Q#: [Question text]

A#: [Your answer - what comes to mind]

Resistance: [0-10, how difficult was this to answer?]
- 0 = Easy, flowed naturally
- 5 = Moderate difficulty
- 10 = Extremely difficult, nearly couldn't answer

Confidence: [0-10, how sure are you this is accurate?]
- 0 = Complete uncertainty
- 5 = Moderate confidence
- 10 = Completely certain
```
