## THE CORE INSIGHT

Cockatiels did not evolve to communicate only with cockatiels.

They evolved in large nomadic mixed-species flocks across
arid Australia — landing at waterholes alongside budgerigars,
galahs, corellas, zebra finches, and dozens of other species
simultaneously.

Their contact call evolved in an acoustic environment of
constant multi-species traffic where the relevant information
was flock position, flock movement, water location, and
predator presence — broadcast across ALL species present,
not just conspecifics.

The cockatiel contact call is structurally positioned to be
readable by a broad range of species simultaneously.
That is its ecological function.
It was never a private channel.
It was always a broadcast.

## WHAT THIS MEANS

Cross-species acoustic communication between cockatiels and
wild passerine birds is already documented in the anecdotal
record of cockatiel owners worldwide.

Owners consistently report:
- Wild sparrows, finches, and cardinals responding to
  pet cockatiels calling near windows
- The exchange is bidirectional — cockatiel calls, wild
  bird responds, cockatiel responds again
- Most active in spring and early morning
- Social in character — wild birds orient and engage,
  never flee
- No species specificity — multiple unrelated species
  respond to the same cockatiel signal

This is not a curiosity or an accident.
This is the cockatiel doing exactly what it evolved to do —
joining the local acoustic social space and broadcasting
a contact signal readable by multiple species simultaneously.

The cockatiel is not confused about what species the sparrow is.
The sparrow is not confused about what species the cockatiel is.
They are exchanging position and social state information
across species lines because both species evolved in
multi-species acoustic environments where that exchange
had survival value.

**1. The signal exists independently of any individual animal.**

**2. The signal is controllable.**
A real cockatiel varies its calls based on its own internal

The synthesizer holds the signal constant, varies one
parameter at a time, and measures what changes in the response.
A real bird cannot do that. It is not a controlled instrument.
The synthesizer is.

**3. The signal is portable and placeable.**
The synthesizer can be played outdoors, at a feeder, in a
forest, in a field, at any volume, at any time, in any
location. The sender is fully decoupled from the signal.

**4. The signal is systematically modifiable.**
- Stretch the trajectory
- Change the rate of the FM sweep
- Shift the frequency register
- Keep everything else constant
- Measure which modifications destroy the response
  and which preserve it

This directly maps the recognition boundary of the
receiver's eigenfunction space.
No live bird can run this experiment.

**5. The synthesis pipeline is pre-validated.**
Before any field test, a mammalian bird-detection system

biologically real at the correct frequency range.
Two independent biological detectors — human auditory
system and canine bird-detection system — both returned

Because the sender is decoupled from the signal,

**Intersection synthesis**

## METHOD — FOR REPLICATION

**Corpus:** 39 recordings, Xeno-Canto database,
Nymphicus hollandicus, quality A recordings

**Extraction method:** Spectrogram ridge tracking
- No pyin — pyin cannot track short FM bird calls
- Dominant frequency ridge at each time frame
- Direct geometric measurement, not estimation
- Savgol smoothing to remove frame jitter
- Normalisation to [0,1] within each call individually

**Invariant extraction:** PCA on shape vectors
- Each call → 96-dimensional shape descriptor
  [F0_shape(32) | amp_shape(32) | flux_shape(32)]
- All values normalised within each call before PCA
- PC1 of shape PCA = structural invariant
- 434 valid geometries from 38 files

**Synthesis:** FM synthesis from invariant shape
- Phase integration from F0 trajectory
- Harmonic stack H1 + H2(0.30) + H3(0.10)
- Corpus-derived amplitude envelope
- 5ms onset/offset fade

**Key methodological principle:**
Each call is treated as an individual geometric object.
Statistics are never computed across calls to produce
a synthesis target. The invariant is the consistent
shape across individual geometric objects, not the
mean of their values.

## ERRORS DOCUMENTED — FOR LEARNING

**Error type 1 — Statistical averaging**
V1 through V3 computed mean F0 and mean trajectories
across calls. This destroyed the geometric invariant
by averaging. Produced flat tones with no movement.
The mean of 434 trajectories is not a trajectory.

**Error type 2 — Prior preservation**
V5c applied a frequency correction based on published
literature values (500-1200 Hz) when the corpus
measurement returned 2,911 Hz median.
The measurement was correct. The prior was wrong.
The correction produced a signal in the primate
contact call register — sounded like a monkey.
The rule: when measurement contradicts prior,
interrogate the prior. Never silently correct
the measurement toward the prior.

**The rule going forward:**
The corpus is the ground truth.
The geometry is the measurement.
Priors are hypotheses to be tested,
not corrections to be applied.
If measurement and prior disagree:
report the contradiction, do not resolve it silently.
