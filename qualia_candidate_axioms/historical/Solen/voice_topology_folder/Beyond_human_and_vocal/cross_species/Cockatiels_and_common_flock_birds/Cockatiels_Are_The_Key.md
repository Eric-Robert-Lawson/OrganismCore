# WHAT WAS BUILT

A synthesizer that produces the geometric invariant
of the cockatiel contact call from first principles.

## The Method

1. 39 quality-A recordings from Xeno-Canto
2. 434 individual calls extracted
3. Each call treated as an individual geometric object
4. Spectrogram ridge tracking — direct measurement
   of F0 trajectory at each time frame
   No pyin. No averaging. No statistics.
5. Each call represented as three normalised shapes:
   - F0 shape (pitch trajectory, scale-free)
   - Amplitude shape (dynamic contour, scale-free)
   - Spectral flux shape (timbral movement, scale-free)
6. PCA on the shapes — not the values
   PC1 = the geometric pattern most consistent
   across all 434 calls
   That is the structural invariant.
7. Synthesis re-scales the invariant shape back
   into absolute acoustic values
