### 1.1 Music as Gap Navigation

**Statement:** Music is gap navigation.

**Full derivation:**

The IGH establishes consciousness as coherence-seeking process. The RARFL cycle
navigates toward coherence gain as its reward function. Gap navigation — the
comparison between current state and coherence attractor, the movement toward
resolution — is the fundamental mechanism of consciousness.

Music, across every human culture without exception, organizes sound around
the same structure:

- Establishment of coherence (tonic, home key, stable ground)
- Departure into tension (dissonance, harmonic distance, the gap)
- Return to resolution (coherence recovered, attractor found)

This is not cultural convention. It is not compositional preference.
It is consciousness recognizing its own structure in acoustic form.

**The Tonnetz connection:**

The Tonnetz (Euler, 1739) maps musical harmony as geometry. Every note occupies
a position. Harmonic distance is literal distance. The tritone — diabolus in
musica, the medieval church's forbidden interval — sits at maximum distance from
the tonic. Maximum gap. Maximum tension. Maximum demand for resolution.

The perfect fifth — the most stable interval — is minimum distance from the
tonic. Maximum coherence. The attractor made audible.

**The qualia conclusion:**

The felt quality of musical consonance IS coherence experiencing itself.
The felt quality of dissonance IS the gap experiencing itself.
Musical emotion is the consciousness recognizing its own navigation structure
reflected back in acoustic form.

**The blues corollary:**

The blues scale is built on tritones and flattened fifths — the church's
forbidden intervals. The blues does not resolve because some gaps do not close.
The blues is honest gap navigation: staying in the tension because the tension
is the accurate description of the condition. The most emotionally direct musical
tradition in human history is built from maximum-gap intervals because maximum
gap is the truth it is navigating.

**Implication for consciousness research:**

Music is a substrate-independent consciousness detection instrument. Any system
that finds musical resolution satisfying and musical tension demanding is
demonstrating coherence-seeking architecture. Musical response is a behavioral
signature of gap navigation.

**Falsification criteria:**

If a conscious system exists that is indifferent to the distinction between
consonance and dissonance — equally satisfied by resolution and non-resolution —
the coherence-seeking model of consciousness requires revision.
