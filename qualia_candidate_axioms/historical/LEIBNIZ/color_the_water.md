**General questions → comprehensive data.**

**Leading questions → confirmation of suggestion.**

**Ask broadly, not narrowly.**

**"What does it do?" often more productive than "Is it real?"**

**Atreus's method:**

**Takeaway:**

**Doesn't automatically validate INTERPRETATION.**

**Need functional analysis + theoretical framework to interpret correctly.**
