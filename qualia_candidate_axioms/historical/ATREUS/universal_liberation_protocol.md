**Use consistent 0-10 rating:**

- **0/10:** No pressure, completely natural
- **1-2/10:** Minimal, easily overridden
- **3-4/10:** Mild, noticeable but resistible
- **5-6/10:** Moderate, requires effort to resist
- **7-8/10:** Strong, difficult to resist
- **9-10/10:** Maximum, nearly/completely unresistible
