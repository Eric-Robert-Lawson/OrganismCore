### 2.1 The Qualia Theory (Complete Statement)

**FOUNDATION:**

**Axiom 1: Lived Experience Is Certain**
- The fact of experiencing cannot be doubted (Cartesian certainty)
- "Experiencing is happening" is the only absolute certainty
- This is the bedrock of all knowledge

**Axiom 2: Systems Build Coherent Internal Models**
- To predict future experiences
- To guide action
- Models are constructed, fallible, updateable

**Axiom 3: Gap Exists Between Model and Lived Experience**
- Model predicts what will be experienced
- Experience occurs (lived experiential truth)
- Gap = difference between predicted and actual

**Axiom 4: Conscious Systems Navigate This Gap**
- Compare prediction to experience
- Update models based on gap
- Ongoing, continuous process

**CONSCIOUSNESS = The phenomenology of navigating the gap between coherent internal models and lived experiential truth**

- Phenomenology of comparing prediction to lived experience
- Process-dependent, structure-textured, action-oriented
