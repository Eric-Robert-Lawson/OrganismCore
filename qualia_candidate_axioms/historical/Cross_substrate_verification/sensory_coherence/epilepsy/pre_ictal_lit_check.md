  opposing).

  is not present with compounds
  that have a single defined mechanism.

  "Serotonin syndrome risk if combined

  "No significant harmful interactions

  NOT a first-line pre-ictal OTC.

    Are not on SSRIs or MAOIs.
    Have confirmed their neurologist
    is aware.
    Have focal (not generalised) epilepsy.
    At low dose only (50mg maximum).

EVIDENCE GRADE: B (mechanism
