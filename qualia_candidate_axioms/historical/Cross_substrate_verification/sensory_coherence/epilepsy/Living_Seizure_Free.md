  Build the pre-sleep routine.
  Eliminate screens 60 minutes
  before bed.
  Target 7-9 hours.
  Hold the timing on weekends.

  Why third:
    Sleep normalisation is
    largely about habit formation.
    It does not require external
    resources or social navigation.
    It requires consistency.
    Establish consistency before
    dietary change.

PHASE 4 — WEEK 8+:
DIETARY TRANSITION

  Begin ketogenic or low-carbohydrate
  dietary transition.
  Work with a registered dietitian
  if available.

  MCT-augmented low-carb diet.
  The MCT C8 in the daily compound
  is already providing mild ketosis.
  The dietary change deepens it.

  Why last:
    Dietary change is the most
    socially complex intervention.
    Attempting it when caffeine
    withdrawal, sleep disruption,
    and supplement habits are
    already resolved means it
    gets full attention.
