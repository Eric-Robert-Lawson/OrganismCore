## THE SOLUBILITY MAP

```

WATER-SOLUBLE COMPOUNDS

    maintained if dosing is consistent.

    LIMITATION: High dose → diarrhea.
    Optimal: multiple smaller doses

    Water-soluble ketone acid.
    Absorbed rapidly — peak plasma
    30-60 minutes.

    BBB MCT transporters saturate
    at plasma BHB of ~4-6 mM.
    Above this: no additional
    brain delivery.
    Half-life: 1-3 hours.
    IMPLICATION: BHB cannot be
    "loaded" once daily for
    sustained brain effect.
    It needs multiple doses
    throughout the day, OR

    a ketogenic metabolic state

    Can be dosed once or twice daily
    for sustained effect.
    ADVANTAGE: Genuinely sustained
    effect from twice-daily dosing.

  Creatine monohydrate
    Water-soluble.
    3-5g/day maintenance.
    Absorbed in gut, transported
    to tissues including brain.

    optimise from supplementation.
    Once optimised: maintained
    by daily dosing.
    Half-life: tissue stores are
    stable over days.
    ADVANTAGE: Can be dosed once
    daily at maintenance. Effect
    is persistent once established.
```
