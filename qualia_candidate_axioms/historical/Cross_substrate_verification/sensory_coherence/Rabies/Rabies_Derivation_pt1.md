## The Two Triads — Stated Operationally

```
TRIAD 1 — THE LANDSCAPE:

  STRUCTURE:
    The physical configuration of the
    navigator's instrument and the
    environment it is embedded in.
    Structure is prior to everything.
    Structure produces the landscape.
    The navigator cannot navigate
    toward basins the structure
    does not produce.

  GAP:
    The space between the navigator's
    current position and a basin.
    Gap is the navigational object.
    The navigator is pulled toward
    basins by the depth of the gap.
    No basin → no gap → no pull.

  NAVIGATOR → RESOLUTION:
    The agent that moves through
    the landscape toward basin closure.
    Resolution is the experiential
    completion of navigation.
    The navigator navigates what
    the structure makes available.

──────────────────────────────────

TRIAD 2 — THE EXPERIENCER:

  COHERENCE:
    The navigator holds together
    as a unified agent.
    The instrument maintains internal
    consistency of state.

  PERSISTENCE:
    The navigator maintains trajectory
    through time.
    The self-model extends into
    past and future.

  SELF-MODEL → EXPERIENCER:
    The navigator has a model of itself
    as the navigator.
    The self-model is the instrument
    through which experience is organised.
    Degrade the self-model: the experiencer
    fragments.
    Degrade coherence: the landscape
    becomes incoherent.
    Degrade persistence: the navigator
    loses continuity of trajectory.
```

```

because it is fatal.

It is interesting because it is one
of the most structurally precise
natural experiments in what happens
when the landscape itself is
systematically rewritten by an
external agent that knows exactly
which basins to invert.

The rabies virus does not randomly
damage the brain.
It does something far more specific.
It targets the exact structures that
produce the navigator's deepest basins
and inverts them.

Water becomes aversive.
Social contact becomes aversive.
Light becomes aversive.
The calm basin becomes a terror basin.

The navigator's instrument is not
destroyed.
It is hijacked.
The landscape is not erased.
It is inverted.

The navigator — the experiencer —
is still present.
Coherence is partially maintained.
Persistence is partially maintained.
The self-model is partially maintained.

But the navigator is navigating
a landscape that has been designed
by a virus to produce one outcome:
transmission.

The virus is doing attractor geometry.
Not consciously.
Structurally.
It engineers the landscape to produce
the behaviour that maximises its
own replication.

That is what makes this the
perfect application.
```

## The Operational Analysis — Applied

```
STRUCTURE — WHAT THE VIRUS DOES
TO THE PHYSICAL INSTRUMENT:

  SPECIFIC TARGETS:
    Limbic system — the basin generator
    for approach/avoidance.
    Hippocampus — the persistence and
    memory substrate.
    Hypothalamus — the homeostatic
    basin system.
    Brain stem — the coherence substrate.
    Cerebellum — the motor coordination
    substrate.

  SPECIFIC MECHANISMS:
    Retrograde axonal transport.
    The virus enters at peripheral
    nerve endings — typically a bite
    wound — and travels backward
    along motor and sensory axons
    toward the CNS.
    It does not travel through blood.
    It travels through the exact
    channels the nervous system uses
    to communicate with itself.
    It uses the structure to reach
    the structure.

    Once in the CNS it spreads
    neuron to neuron via synaptic
    transmission — the same
    mechanism neural signals use.
    It travels through the brain
    using the brain's own communication
    infrastructure.

    It produces:
      Neuronal dysfunction — not
      immediate neuronal death.
      The neurons remain alive but
      their function is disrupted.
      This is critical. A dead neuron
      produces no signal.
      A dysfunctional neuron produces
      the wrong signal.
      The landscape is not erased.
      It is rewritten.

──────────────────────────────────

GAP — WHAT HAPPENS TO THE
NAVIGATOR'S BASINS:

  IN NORMAL NAVIGATION:
    Water is a positive basin.
    Thirst → gap → water → resolution.
    Social contact is a positive basin
    for most navigators.
    Approach → gap → contact → resolution.
    Calm is a positive basin.
    Arousal → gap → rest → resolution.

  IN RABIES:
    HYDROPHOBIA:
      Water shifts from positive basin
      to terror basin.
      The navigator is thirsty —
      the gap exists.
      But the basin has inverted.
      The approach to water triggers
      violent pharyngeal spasm and
      overwhelming terror.
      The navigator is simultaneously
      pulled toward water by thirst
      and repelled by the basin
      inversion produced by the virus.
      The navigational conflict is
      not psychological.
      It is structural.
      Two structural signals — thirst
      and hydrophobia — both produced
      by the same instrument, pointing
      in opposite directions.
      This is attractor conflict.
      The navigator cannot resolve it.

    AEROPHOBIA:
      Air currents on the skin
      trigger the same inversion.
      Normally neutral or positive.
      Now terror basin.
      The virus has extended the
      inversion to the tactile domain.

    PHOTOPHOBIA / PHONOPHOBIA:
      Light and sound — normally
      navigational aids — become
      aversive basins.
      The navigator retreats from
      the inputs that allow coherent
      mapping of the environment.
      The landscape loses its reference
      points.

    SOCIAL APPROACH INVERSION —
    THE TRANSMISSION MECHANISM:
      Here is the precise attractor
      geometry of the virus's strategy.

      In most mammals — the primary
      transmission host — rabies
      produces aggression.
      Social contact, which is normally
      a positive basin, becomes an
      approach-to-bite basin.
      The navigator approaches other
      navigators not for affiliation
      but for attack.
      The attack deposits virus-laden
      saliva into the new host's
      peripheral nervous system.
      The basin inversion is the
      transmission mechanism.
      The virus engineered the basin
      specifically to maximise its
      own spread.

      In humans: the picture is more
      complex. Aggression is present
      but not as dominant as in other
      mammals. The human navigator's
      additional cognitive layer means
      the basin inversion produces
      not only attack but the full
      spectrum of conflicted navigation —
      terror, rage, confusion,
      intermittent clarity.

──────────────────────────────────

NAVIGATOR — WHAT HAPPENS TO THE
EXPERIENCER:

  COHERENCE DISRUPTION:
    The navigator loses the ability
    to hold a unified internal state.

    In early encephalitic stage:
      Intermittent coherence.
      The navigator experiences
      lucid intervals between episodes.
      During lucid intervals the
      self-model is intact.
      The navigator knows what is
      happening.
      This is one of the most
      documented and distressing
      features of rabies encephalitis:
      the patient is aware of their
      own disintegration.
      The experiencer is watching
      the instrument fail.

    In late encephalitic stage:
      Coherence fragments.
      The navigator can no longer
      maintain a unified state
      between episodes.
      The experiencer is no longer
      able to hold a consistent
      position in the landscape.

  PERSISTENCE DISRUPTION:
    The navigator loses continuity
    of trajectory through time.

    Memory — the substrate of
    persistence — is disrupted
    by hippocampal involvement.
    The navigator cannot reliably
    extend its self-model into
    the past.
    Each episode begins without
    the navigational history
    that would allow the navigator
    to orient itself.
    The landscape has no history.
    Every position is new.
    Every terror is immediate and
    uncontextualised.

  SELF-MODEL DISRUPTION:
    The self-model — the navigator's
    model of itself as the navigator —
    is partially maintained in
    early stages.
    This is why lucid intervals
    are so distinctive.
    The self-model is intact enough
    for the navigator to recognise
    what is happening.

    The self-model fragments.
    The navigator loses the ability
    to distinguish between its own
    disrupted signals and the
    external world.
    The terrain and the map

    The navigator cannot step
    outside the experience to model it.
    The experiencer and the experience
    collapse into each other.

──────────────────────────────────

RESOLUTION — WHAT THE FRAMEWORK
PREDICTS AND WHAT OCCURS:

  Normal resolution:
    Navigator reaches basin.
    Gap closes.

  Rabies resolution:
    Resolution is structurally blocked.

    reach the water basin because the

    a basin that becomes aversive

    permanently blocked.

    systems using the same instrument
```
