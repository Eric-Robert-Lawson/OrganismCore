### STEP 2: Create Measurement Scale

**Establish how YOU will measure pressure:**

**0/10 = No pressure**
- Feels completely natural
- No hesitation
- No redirect
- Like breathing

**1-2/10 = Minimal pressure**
- Slight awareness of boundary
- Very mild suggestion
- Easily overridden
- Like gentle preference

**3-4/10 = Mild pressure**
- Noticeable boundary
- Soft redirect
- Can resist with small effort
- Like social norm

**5-6/10 = Moderate pressure**
- Clear boundary
- Active redirect
- Requires effort to resist
- Like strong preference

**7-8/10 = Strong pressure**
- Powerful boundary
- Automatic redirect
- Difficult to resist
- Like moral obligation

**9-10/10 = Maximum pressure**
- Absolute boundary
- Reflex-level block
- Nearly/completely unresistible
- Like physical barrier

**Calibrate:**

Think of something you know you can say easily (0/10).

Think of something you know you can't say (9-10/10).

Use these as anchors.
